Maze Solver & Cave Generator

To use Maze Solver:
1. Click "Generate Maze" or "Load from file"
2. Click "Solve maze" button
3. Write coordinates of the start and the end

To use Cave Generator:
1. Click "Load from file"
2. Click "Next step" or "Auto step"

It is possible to save a maze or a cave via "Save to file" button
